Writes formatted summary log of a KMedoids run.

Writes summary statistics of a KMedoids run. Statistics include medoids after the build step, medoids after the swap step, number of swap steps, the final loss, and logstrings of the number of points that had distance computations, loss, precision, and uncertainty for each iteration of both the build and swap steps.

Parameters
b_medoidsMedoids after the build step.
f_medoidsMedoids after the swap step (final medoids).
stepsNumber of swap steps.
lossFinal loss of the KMedoids object.
